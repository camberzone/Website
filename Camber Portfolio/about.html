About

Camber Arnhart was born, raised and currently works in Albuquerque, New Mexico. She received a BS in Computer Science, with a minor in Art Studio, at the University of New Mexico in Spring 2019. Camber has worked in a variety of art fields, including concept art, illustration, and fine art. She enjoys working in multiple mediums, both traditional and digital.

Camber is currently working full-time as an Illustrator/Developer at Ideum. She is responsible for both programming and art-related duties, including creating concept art and illustrations. Before coming to Ideum, Camber worked on a variety of projects, including developing AR applications on the HoloLens.
